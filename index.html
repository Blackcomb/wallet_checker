WHAT THIS SITE DOES

You give this site a Bitcoin address, and it'll check the Blockchain to see what tranactions are associated with that address. You can even see a running total.

CAVEATS

While this site accurately shows you how many BTC are in a given address, it's not an accurate representation of how many BTC someone has. After every bitcoin transaction there is additional "change" which the sender then sends back to themself at a separate address.
